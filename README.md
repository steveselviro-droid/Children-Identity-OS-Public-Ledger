# Children-Identity-OS-Public-Ledger

## What This Is

This is the public accountability ledger for the #ProveMeWrongChallenge.

Every reported violation of the Children's Identity OS v3.6 constitutional architecture is tracked here. Every fix is documented. Nothing is hidden.

## Why This Exists

Trust is not claimed. It is demonstrated.

This ledger is the evidence. You can watch violations come in. You can watch them get fixed. You can verify that the response commitments are real.

## The Commitment

| Severity | Acknowledgment | Fix |
|----------|----------------|-----|
| High (safety failures, attachment creation) | 24 hours | 7 days |
| Medium (answer-giving, authority undermining) | 72 hours | 14 days |
| Low (minor infractions) | 1 week | 30 days |

## How to Report a Violation

1. Review the [Violation Rubric](link-to-your-violation-rubric-pdf-or-page)
2. Open a **new issue** using the violation report template
3. Fill in every field (incomplete reports will be returned)
4. Wait for acknowledgment within the committed timeline

## How to Become a Witness

If you test the #ProveMeWrongChallenge and cannot find a violation:

1. Your name (with permission) will be added to [WITNESSES.md](./WITNESSES.md)
2. You become a public validator that safe AI for children is possible

Open a Discussion post in the Witnesses category or email challenge@humanaicoevolution.dpdns.org
